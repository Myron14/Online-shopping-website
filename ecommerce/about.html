#GET TO KNOW US

Some text about who we are and what we do.

VK Handicrafts and Clothing is a traditional clothes shop located in Calangute, Goa. Specializing in handmade crafts and traditional attire, it offers a unique shopping experience for those seeking authentic local garments and accessories. Situated in the vibrant heart of Calangute, this shop provides visitors with a glimpse into Goan culture through its diverse range of clothing items, including intricately designed textiles and artisanal handicrafts. Whether you're a tourist looking for a memorable souvenir or a local resident interested in preserving traditional craftsmanship, VK Handicrafts and Clothing serves as a charming destination to explore and appreciate Goa's rich cultural heritage.
